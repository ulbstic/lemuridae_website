Lemuridae

Text Mining for Information Governance

Mission

Lemuridae provides methods and tools to offer a resting ground for the disturbed souls of documents, condemned to wander in the meanders of shared drives.

Case Studies

There is an urgent need for empirically-based research in the domain of information governance. Lemuridae focusses on aggregating real-life case-studies.

Team

Lemuridae is an initiative of MaSTIC (Université libre de Bruxelles) and iMinds (Universiteit Gent), based in the very heart of Europe.
