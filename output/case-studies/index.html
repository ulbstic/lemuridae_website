Case Studies

There is an urgent need for empirically-based research in the domain of information governance. Academics and standards bodies often issue guidelines without acknowledging the reality of the terrain. On the other hand, the quality of auto-classification tools provided by vendors can not be taken at face value. Lemuridae therefore focusses on aggregating real-life case-studies from the corporate and public sector in which the possibilities and limits of automated metadata creation are analysed in an objective and scientific manner.
